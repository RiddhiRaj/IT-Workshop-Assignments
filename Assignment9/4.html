Elon Musk: Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future is a 2015 biography of entrepreneur Elon Musk by Ashlee Vance. The book was published by Ecco, an imprint of HarperCollins, on June 16, 2015. The book was a New York Times bestseller for 12 weeks

Ashlee Vance is an American journalist and author. He is the author of Elon Musk: Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future, a biography of Elon Musk, and In the Plex: How Google Thinks, Works, and Shapes Our Lives, a book about Google. Vance is a contributing editor at Wired magazine.
